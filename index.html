About me

I have a passion for all things creative.

I have just finished my third year majoring in Industrial Design and Music at the University of Notre Dame.

I love to work on creative detail-oriented projects. I love taking on multiple creative projects at one time. I love partaking in any artistic project and have a passion for many creative outlets (including ceramics, glass blowing, painting, drawing, sculpting). I also love working with teams to complete a project that cannot be completed by one person alone. I am also currently learning to use powerful technological tools to expand my creative potential (3d modeling and web design).
